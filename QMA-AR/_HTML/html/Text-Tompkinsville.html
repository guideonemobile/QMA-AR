Tompkinsville – St.George, Staten Island

Located in the northeastern section of Staten Island, Tompkinsville was famous during colonial times for its fresh spring water. Today the neighborhood boasts an active literary and arts scene while at the same time fostering one of the largest Sri Lankan communities outside of Sri Lanka.
